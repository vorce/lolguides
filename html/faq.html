FAQ

Frequently asked questions with answers. To ask anything about the site; use e-mail, g+ or twitter.

Q: Why are only guides from clgaming.net and solomid.net and lolpro.com aggregated on this site?

A: Because they are the two, for now, sites with the most up to date and high quality, in depth guides. I'm however open to add new source sites if they are deemed good enough.

Q: What does gray star and gold star mean?

A: Gray star means the guide is not featured on its source site. Gold star means it is featured.

Q: There are no good guides listed for my favorite champ X?!

A: Then there probably isn't any good rated guides available for champion X on either solomid nor clgaming. Sorry! (There's also the possibility that my scraping has a bug - in that case feel free to contact me)

Q: Who created lolguides.net?

A: Me!

Q: There's no section or any guides yet for the new champion Y!?

A: Don't worry, it is probably on its way. It takes a while to add a new section, icon etc for me. Then it of course take a bit of time for people to actually try the champion and write up a decent guide for it. Hang in there.

Q: Why the ads?

A: I put them up to hopefully cover hosting costs. If you don't see them pretty please turn off your adblocker for lolguides.net! :-)

Q: Why doesn't my guide, which is up on site Z show up on lolguides?

A: It's rating is probably too low. No guides with sub zero rating will be listed on the top guides section. The latest guides section may list guides with a sub zero rating though.
